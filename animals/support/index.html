animals is maintained as an open, ongoing record.

The record began in April 1998, with drawings made over the following years. Some of these works were shown publicly in limited exhibitions, including a gallery exhibition in 2005 and a selected entry in a design competition in 2007.

After a long period of inactivity, the record has been observed again since 2026, with a small number of new entries added gradually.

This page exists only to describe how that record is sustained.

About support

Support functions solely as a way to help keep the record publicly available — to maintain the conditions under which observation and recording can continue.

Support does not define meaning.
It does not shape direction.
It does not accelerate production.

Nothing more is promised.
Nothing else is required.

What this support sustains

Support is applied to the practical continuity of animals as a publicly accessible record over time.

The record is not hosted as a finished work, nor as a project with a planned conclusion. It is placed in an environment intended for long-term persistence beyond individual presence.

The same observer also maintains other independent records* that exist alongside animals, following similar principles of quiet documentation.

Each record remains separate.
No unified message or conclusion is intended.
