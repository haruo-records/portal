Haruo

Haruo is an observer.

animals is not presented as finished artwork or a message.
What you see here are traces of observation — recorded as they appeared, without interpretation.

Haruo is only one observation point.
Every observation is shaped by time, culture, body, and experience.
If someone else were to observe animals, something different might appear.

This project does not aim to explain, define, or conclude.
It continues by observing and recording, and by leaving space for what cannot be fixed into meaning.

You do not need knowledge of art to engage with this work.
You do not need to understand it.
Its existence, as an ongoing record, is enough.

About support

animals exists through continuous observation and recording.

Support is not a purchase, and not agreement with an interpretation.
It is a way to help keep this observation ongoing.

If you feel that this activity should continue —
that it should remain present, even quietly — that is sufficient reason.
